How to Play Letter Boxed

Letter Boxed is a word game where you aim to connect letters across four sides of a square box to form words, ultimately using all twelve letters.

  • Form words by connecting letters from the four sides of the box
  • The last letter of one word must be the first letter of the next word
  • You cannot use two letters from the same side consecutively
  • All 12 letters must be used to solve the puzzle
  • The goal is to solve the puzzle in as few words as possible, typically two or three words

Tips & Strategies

Improve your Letter Boxed skills with these strategies:

  • Focus on long words to cover many letters in one go
  • Try to end your first word with a letter that allows you to start the next word on a different side and cover as many remaining letters as possible
  • Look for common letter patterns, prefixes, and suffixes
  • Practice making connections between sides, especially using letters in the center of a side
  • Aim for the two-word solution, which is the most efficient way to win
