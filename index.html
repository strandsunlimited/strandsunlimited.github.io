How to Play Strands

Strands is a word search game where you need to find words that fit the day's theme. Look for the spangramâ€”a special word that spans across the entire grid and relates to the theme.

  • Find theme words related to the daily topic
  • Discover the spangram that spans the entire grid
  • Words can be found in any direction: up, down, left, right, or diagonal
  • Letters can be reused in different words
  • Complete the puzzle by finding all theme words and the spangram

Improve your Strands gameplay with these expert tips:

  • Start by looking for the most obvious theme words
  • The spangram usually relates closely to the theme
  • Look for prefixes and suffixes that might help find related words
  • Don't forget to check diagonal directions
  • Use the hint button if you're stuck on the last few words
