Strands Unlimited Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

Strands Unlimited ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, including any other media form, media channel, mobile website, or mobile application related or connected thereto (collectively, the "Site"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Site.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect limited information necessary for the function of the games and site analytics:

  • Local Gameplay Data: We use local storage (like cookies) in your browser to save your game progress, settings (like theme preference), and statistics (like scores and streaks). This data is stored only on your device and is not collected by us.
  • Google Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to collect general usage data, such as which pages you visit, how long you stay, and your device type. This is anonymous and aggregated data used to improve the Site.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to access or store information. Specific information about how we use such technologies and how you can refuse certain cookies is set out in our Cookie Policy (below).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information collected to:

  • Manage your gameplay (e.g., save and load progress).
  • Improve the performance, accessibility, and user experience of the Site.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience.
  • Provide targeted advertisements (via third-party services) to keep the service free.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected in certain situations. Your information may be disclosed as follows:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: If we believe the release of information is necessary to respond to legal process, to investigate or remedy potential violations of our policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others, we may share your information as permitted or required by any applicable law, rule, or regulation.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third parties that perform services for us or on our behalf, including data analysis, advertising, email delivery, hosting services, and customer service.

4. Third-Party Websites and Advertising

The Site contains third-party advertisements and links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these third parties. Please review the policies of any third-party websites or services you interact with.

5. Policy for Children

We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If you become aware of any data we have collected from children under age 13, please contact us using the information provided below.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by a revised "Last Updated" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy frequently to be informed of how we are protecting your information.
